Steve Kain came out of Bridgeton, New Jersey — a town so historic they haven't gotten around to updating it since 1970. He is, by a large margin, the most popular entertainer to ever come from there. This is not disputed by anyone who matters.

He has performed in front of dozens of fans in over 3 states. He won Atlantic City's Funniest. He has never lost a roast battle. Never will. Any place, any time. Just put his name on a flyer and he'll be there. He's still waiting for somebody to actually challenge him on this.

Music is his long time foe and nemesis, so naturally he started doing comedy music. If you answered yes to at least 2 out of 3 of the following — Do you like comedy? Do you like music? Do you like comedy music? — then this is for you. If you answered no to any of them, this show is not for you.

He's also a published poet. Over 100 poems in various literary magazines. Yes, really. He contains multitudes. He also did the Mummers Parade. Super glued a costume together in an hour. It held up. Mostly.

Steve performs constantly. Multiple shows a week across Philly, South Jersey, and wherever else they'll have him. Every show is a once-in-a-lifetime historic event. A set that will never be repeated. He'll just keep doing this thing until the wheels fall off.

Get out of your stupid house and come see a show. How many friends did you make last night? None. That's what I thought.
